Journey in Photography

It started with this technical course I had in school called DMAD. One of the things we did was to do photography. I bought my still current camera from Canon's SM Aura branch and it all went from there. I've been very into just taking photos of still objects but I have been more outgoing as of recent to try to capture candid street photography and events.
